i've come across <b>Exalted by Anna Dorn</b> over a year ago, in a now-defunct secondhand bookshop in manchester city center (rest in peace) and i felt like that in itself was the sign from the universe. someone <i>wanted</i> me to read this book. the fatalism of this (of my time in manchester, actually) was part of the reason this book had come to mind once again. a timely entrance for pride month. 

this book kind of reads like someone's instagram brainrots (or one of those shitpost accounts that only post vague memes with recycled chinese langugage copypastas in their descriptions), in all the cringeworthy, awkward, and funny glory. fitting when you know that the book takes us around the orbit of an astrology instagram account runner, and probably one of the most sought after population group of all time: a partnerless middle-aged lesbian. which i found to be the only unbelievable thing in the entire book. 

### <b>awards:</b> 
the jennifer coolidge award for best milf of all time (sorry carol!) 

### <b>for a fan of:</b> 
- torrey peters
- milf category trawlers
- astrology queer (or queer adjacent) babies
